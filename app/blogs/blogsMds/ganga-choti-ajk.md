## Choosing the Right Vehicle

- **SUV or 4x4:** Recommended for hilly roads and steep inclines near Ganga Choti.
- **Sedans:** Suitable if starting from main highways and sticking to well-paved roads.
- **Minivans:** For family or group trips with luggage.

---

## Suggested Route

**Islamabad → Muzaffarabad → Bagh → Ganga Choti Base Camp**

- Distance: ~210 km from Islamabad (5–6 hours drive).
- Roads: Mix of highways and hilly paths.
- Highlights: River views, forests, and local villages.

**Tip:** Start early in the morning to avoid fog and enjoy maximum daylight.

---

## Essential Travel Tips

1. **Book in Advance:** Peak season (May–September) sees heavy tourist traffic.
2. **Check Car Condition:** Tires, brakes, AC, and fuel tank are crucial.
3. **Fuel Up:** Limited fuel stations exist near Bagh and Ganga Choti.
4. **Hire a Local Driver (Optional):** They know narrow and steep mountain roads.
5. **Navigation:** Offline maps recommended; network coverage can be weak.

## Safety Tips

- Avoid night driving in mountainous areas.
- Monitor weather conditions; sudden rainfall or fog can make roads slippery.
- Carry basic first-aid, snacks, and water for the journey.
- Respect local traffic and community rules.
