## Why Book a Car Online?

Online car booking provides several advantages over traditional booking methods:

- **Convenience:** Book anytime, anywhere using mobile or desktop.
- **Instant Confirmation:** Receive confirmation instantly via SMS or email.
- **Transparent Pricing:** Fare estimates are provided upfront; no hidden charges.
- **Variety of Vehicles:** From economy sedans to luxury SUVs and vans.
- **Driver Options:** Verified drivers for safety and reliability.
- **24/7 Customer Support:** Assistance is available at all times.

---

## Step 1: Select Pickup and Drop-off Locations

Choosing the correct pickup and drop-off points is crucial:

- Use exact addresses for accurate fare calculation.
- Include airport terminal details if applicable.
- Specify round-trip or one-way service.

### Pro Tips:

- For airport pickups, add flight numbers to avoid delays.
- For long intercity trips, include multiple drop-off points if necessary.

---

## Step 2: Choose Date and Time

Specifying your pickup date and time ensures vehicle availability:

- Book at least **24 hours in advance** during peak seasons.
- For intercity travel, consider traffic and rest stops.
- Platforms may offer **real-time availability** to reduce waiting time.

---

## Step 3: Choose the Vehicle Type

Vehicle selection depends on trip purpose, number of passengers, and luggage:

### Common Vehicle Options in Pakistan:

1. **Economy Cars:** Suzuki Cultus, Toyota Corolla – budget-friendly.
2. **Mid-Size Sedans:** Honda Civic, Toyota Corolla Altis – ideal for small families.
3. **SUVs:** Honda BR-V, Toyota Fortuner – suitable for rough terrains or long trips.
4. **Luxury Vehicles:** Mercedes, BMW, Audi – premium comfort and style.
5. **Vans / Minibuses:** Toyota Hiace, Suzuki Bolan – for groups or large families.

### Tips:

- Check **fuel efficiency** for longer trips.
- Confirm **seat capacity** vs passengers.
- Ensure enough **luggage space**.

---

## Step 4: Driver or Self-Drive

Most platforms provide two options:

1. **Professional Driver:** For convenience, safety, and local knowledge.
2. **Self-Drive:** For privacy and flexibility.

### Driver Tips:

- Confirm **verified driver ID**.
- Check **reviews and ratings**.
- Special requests available (e.g., female driver or language preferences).

### Self-Drive Tips:

- Ensure **valid license** (Pakistani or international).
- Understand **insurance and liability rules**.
- Review policies for **fuel and mileage**.

---

## Step 5: Booking Confirmation and Payment

After selecting vehicle and driver:

- Review booking details carefully.
- Payment methods: Credit/debit cards, JazzCash, EasyPaisa, Cash on Pickup.
- Always obtain a **digital receipt**.

### Tips:

- Use platforms with **clear cancellation/refund policies**.
- Check for **discount codes or promo offers**.

## Common FAQs

### Q1: Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, most platforms allow cancellations with a refund policy.

### Q2: Is insurance included?

Some bookings include vehicle and driver insurance. Confirm before booking.

### Q3: Can I request a female driver?

Certain platforms allow requests based on gender or language.

### Q4: Who pays for fuel?

Driver-included trips generally include fuel; self-drive trips require you to pay.

### Q5: Can I book intercity trips?

Yes, intercity trips are available, with fare estimates and GPS tracking.

### Q6: What documents are required?

For self-drive, a valid driving license is required. Driver bookings may require ID verification.

### Q7: How far in advance should I book?

At least 24–48 hours in advance for peak seasons or long trips.
