## Must-Visit Places in Muzaffarabad

1. **Red Fort (Chak Fort):** Historic fort offering panoramic views of the city and river.
2. **Pir Chinasi:** Famous for sunrise views, ideal for photography.
3. **Ratti Gali Lake:** High-altitude lake reachable via a combination of drive and trekking.
4. **Neelum River:** Perfect for riverside picnics and scenic drives.
5. **Bagh Road and Leepa Valley:** Drive through lush greenery, waterfalls, and mountain passes.
6. **Danna Meadows:** Open green meadows for camping and hiking.

**Tip:** Renting a car allows easy access to these sites without relying on public transport schedules.

## Travel Tips for Muzaffarabad Trips

1. **Book Cars in Advance:** Especially in peak seasons (April–September).
2. **Vehicle Inspection:** Tires, brakes, and engine must be checked.
3. **Fuel Planning:** Some scenic spots are far from fuel stations.
4. **Local Drivers (Optional):** Useful for narrow or steep roads.
5. **Navigation:** Use GPS or offline maps; network coverage may be limited.
